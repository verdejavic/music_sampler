Cristian Cabrerizo, better known as Cris Cab, is a 23-year-old songwriter and musician from Miami, Florida. Unlike any other, his unique style of music is a fusion of pop, reggae and soul. Cris Cab has worked with a number of highly successful artists, such as Pharrell Williams, Wyclef Jean and Big Sean. His latest single, "Turn Out The Light," is a cross-genre jam featuring J. Balvin.
